Neuralink Technology



The microchips used in Neuralidays were designed by Neuralink which was co-founded by Elon Musk.

He gave the inspiration for a team of researchers that tested the chips in people's brains. Neuralink aims to "connect humans and computers" according to its website.

In 2025, after 7 years of research they managed to successfully implant a human brain with a microchip that allow them to see for the first time their thoughts and dreams.

This has since been refined further with bio-neural feedback technology rendering through powerful immerse VR technology.

Our technology is completely safe and many of our services did not require us to cut open your skull and insert a chip into it!

In fact, our latest Neuralink interface uses a 100% accurate holographic reconstruction of your brain to create the environment and biofeedback.
